Davit Systems

Roof/ Parapet Mounted Low Profile Davit

Low profile davit are usually used for low rise building.The davit bases are anchored to the roof or parapet to hold & support the davit pole & the jib.The davit system is manually transferred to different work positions. Low profile davit system can be used in buildings which has clear passage all around the ground level to transfer the cradle from different working positions.

Roof/ Parapet Mounted High Profile Davit

High profile davits are commonly used for parking the cradle on the roof. High profile davits are also used for buildings, where clear passage at ground level for rigging & derigging the cradle is not available.
