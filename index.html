    Power Machines LLC

    Power Machines LLC was established in the year 1996 in the emirates of Dubai, UAE. Power Machines LLC is a dynamic and Quality driven organization with corporate office located in Ras Al Khor industrial area Dubai and state of art manufacturing facility established in National Industries Park (Formally known as Techno Park), Dubai, UAE.

    With our 21 years of experience in executing all type BMU projected and having completed over 900 major projects. Power Machines has been one stop solution for all consultants, main contractors and building owners

    Fabrication Division has been engaged as specialist contractor for Designing, Supply and Installation of Structural steel including Secondary steel works, Non-Structural Metal Works such as Architectural Metal Products, Storage Tanks, Pressure Vessel and all types of Machining works.

    • QUALITY

      Safety and quality being of prime importance, Power Machines documents, implements and maintains Integrated Quality Management System (IMS) thereby improving quality of end product. Power Machines has been accredited to the ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015, OSHAS 18001:2007 by TUV NORD.

    • MANUFACTURING FACILITIES

      Our manufacturing unit is located at Ras Al Khor, Dubai, U.A.E. Using the latest state-of-the- art equipments Power Machines was one of the first to manufacture building maintenance units in the Middle East. With a highly skilled team of German trained design Engineers and technicians, we can undertake projects of any size and complexity.

    • CUTOM BUILD SYSTEMS

      At Power Machines, our design Engineers using the latest 3D Modelling and structural analysis, design BMU’s for the most architecturally complicated buildings. A triple elbow machine with revolving tracks for Business Central Towers was designed which is one of a kind in the World.
